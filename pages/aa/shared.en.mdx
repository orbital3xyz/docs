# Shared Accounts

Designed for teams and organizations, it supports the creation and import of existing accounts and the aggregation of transaction records.

Shared accounts are the choice for institutions or organizations with high security, high scalability, efficient team collaboration support, and more.
- Works with existing infrastructure, such as hardware wallets
- Collaborative
- No KYC required
- Full autonomy to control on-chain behavior
- Can support professional-level financial functions, such as recurring payments, bulk payments, financial statements, etc.

Shared accounts use multi-signature technology and provide multi-person collaboration on top of that, allowing multiple users to manage an account together, each user can operate on the account, but multiple user signatures are required to complete transactions.
